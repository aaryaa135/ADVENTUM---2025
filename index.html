What is Adventum?

Adventum is the main flagship event of ACM JUIT, featuring hackathons, coding contests, tech quizzes, and fun events. It fosters innovation, problem-solving, and collaboration among tech enthusiasts. With a mix of AI, cybersecurity, and software development, Adventum offers hands-on challenges for all skill levels. The event also sees participation and collaboration from various colleges, making it a diverse and competitive tech fest.

Participants will be building, learning, and creating technology projects from scratch.
